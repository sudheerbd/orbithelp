Exporting a Report

To Export a Report

  1. Logon to the source instance, under the Reports area, select the required folder to view the reports under it.

  2. Right-click the required report, and then click Export.

      (or)                    

For the required report, click icon, and then click Export.


a. To import the reports, you must first import its corresponding domain, only then the reports will get exported from source instance and get imported into the target instance.
b. Here, we have exported and imported “Test Domain -export-import” domain. Now, export the reports under this domain.

 

  1. Export Report dialog appears.

 

  1. To export the report, click Submit.

  2. A success message appears once the report is exported, and a new process is created under Processes under Monitoring.

  3. Select the required process, and its corresponding output appears under Output(s) & Delivery.

  1. Select the required output.

  2. The output gets as an orb file.

  3. Place the orb file of the exported report in an appropriate location on the system.
