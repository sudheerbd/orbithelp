Purging Processes

To purge a process

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to , and then click Jobs.

  2. Jobs page appears with a list of Seeded and Custom jobs.

  3. Under Jobs sections, on Purge Processes, click , and then click Schedule.

  4. Purge Processes dialog appears.     

 

   

 

  1. The Parameters tab appears by default.

  1. Under the Parameters tab, the following options appear:

    1. In the Till Date box, type the till date to delete the processes that are scheduled up to that date.

    2. in the Users box, type the names of the users to delete the processes  that are scheduled by them.

    3. In the Processes box, type/select the number of processes.

    4. In the Week(s) box, type/select the number of weeks.

    5. To purge the report statistics with the above-mentioned criteria (Till Date, Users, and Processes), select the Report Statistics (with above criteria) checkbox.

    6. To purge the collaboration with the above-mentioned criteria (Till Date, Users, and Processes), select the Collaboration (with above criteria) checkbox.

  1. To purge the process, click Submit.
