Adding a Report to Favorites

Procedure 1

To add a report to favorites

 

1. Under Reports section, select the required folder to view the reports available under the selected folder on the right-hand side.

2. Right-click the required report, and then click Add to favorites.  

(OR)

   For the selected report, click  , and then click Add to favorites. 

 

                    

3.  A success message appears.

4. The report appears under Favorites as shown below :

 

5. To remove report from Favorites, right click on the report and then click Remove from favorites.

 

Procedure 2

To view the recently run reports

 

1. In the Home page, click plus icon, and then click Favorites   icon at the bottom-right corner of the page.

2. The favorites reports appear under Favorites section.

 

                               

3. To run a report that appears under Favorites area, click Run report.   

4. To refresh the recent reports, click Refresh (highlighted as “1”). 

5. To close the recent window, click Close panel

Close panel icon will available once user Unlock Home Widget.
