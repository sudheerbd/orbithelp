Assign to Role

This feature assigns the business objects to the available role.

 

To assign BO to Role

 

1.      Under the Assign to Role tab, the available roles and their assigned business objects appear.

2.      To view the assigned role for a business object, select the required role.  

3.      For the selected role, its assigned business object appears under Assigned Business Objects.

4.   To assign a business object for a specific role (highlighted as “1”), click  Search for items to add.

5.      A list of  available business objects appear.

6.      For the selected role, select the required business object (highlighted as “2”) from the list, and then click save.
