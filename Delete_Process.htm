Deleting Process

To delete the process

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to Monitoring, and then click Processes.

  2. Processes page appears with a list of processes.

  3. To delete the process, click  icon  (highlighted as “1”), and then click Delete (highlighted as "2").

  4. A message “Are you sure you want to delete the process"  appears.

  5. To delete the process, click Yes.
