Setting Simple Option

To set the simple option

 

1. On the Simple tab, under the Recurrence area (highlighted as “1”), select any one of the following:

 

 

                

      • Never Repeat

      • Simple Repeat

      • Repeat

      • Calendar Dates

When Repeat is selected, other options— Every, Months, Starting on month, Last day of month— appear.

 

Never Repeat

 

To schedule a report without repeating, select Never Repeat from Recurrence.

  • To view the never repeat, click Evaluate.

Simple Repeat

 "Simple Repeat" option enables the user to schedule the request at a required interval.

  1. To schedule a report with simple repeat, select Simple Repeat from Recurrence.

  2. When Simple Repeat is selected, perform the following:

a.  In the Every box, type/select the required number.

b. In the text box next to Every box, type/select the required option from the following:

    • Months

    • Week Days

    • Days

    • Hours

    • Minutes

    • Years

 

Simple Repeat calculates the next execution from the schedule day of the report.

Here, we are giving the next schedule should be scheduled in every next 5 months from the scheduled date.

  • To view the next execution of simple repeat, click Evaluate.

  • The schedule information appears as shown below:

 

Repeat

"Repeat" option enables the user to schedule the request at a required interval.

  1. To schedule a report with repeat, select Repeat from Recurrence.

  2. When Repeat is selected, perform the following:

a.  In the Every box, type/select the required number.

b. In the text box next to Every box, type/select the required option from the following:

    • Months

    • Week Days

    • Days

    • Hours

    • Minutes

    • Years

 c.  In the Starting on month box, type/select the required day of the month when the report has to be scheduled.

 

The number has to be in between 1 to 12.   

  

As mentioned in the Repeat section the report is going to start schedule on 6th month.
The next execution will be scheduled by every two months from the 6th month.

 

d.  To schedule the report on the last day of the month, select Last day of month check box.

When Last day of month checkbox is selected, it is not needed to enter any number in Starting on month box.

  1. To view the next execution of simple repeat, click Evaluate.
