Assigning Groups to the Role

To assign Groups to the Role

 

  1. Select the required role, and then click  next to Assigned Groups.

  2. A list of groups appears (highlighted as "1") .

 

                                                                 

  1. Select the required group from the list.

  2. Under Assigned Groups, the selected group appears.

 

        

      5. To save the changes, click Save.
