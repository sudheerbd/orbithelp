Renaming a Report

To Rename a Report

 

1. Under the Reports area, select the required folder to view the reports under it.

2. Right-click the required report, and then click Rename.   

      (or)                   

   For the required report, click icon, and then click Rename.

3. Rename- <report name> dialog appears.

 

 

  a.      In the Name box, update the required name.

b.      In the Description box, enter/update the required description.

4. To save the changes, click Save.

5. To close the dialog box, click Cancel.
