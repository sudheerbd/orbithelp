Deleting a Logical Model

To Delete a Logical Model

 

1.     Under Domain Hierarchy, Logical Models folder appears.  

2.     Under the folder, right-click the required logical model, and then click Remove.

          

 

    

                                  

3.A message " Are you sure you want to delete <Logical Model Name> ?" appears.   

4. To delete the logical model, click Yes.

5. To cancel the action, click No or ..
