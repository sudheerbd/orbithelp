Quick Filter

Since one report can have multiple quick filters, it is essential to manage the reports.

 

To Manage the Quick Filter

 

  1. For the required report, on the top right-hand corner, click Options, and then click Quick Filter.

  2. Quick Filters dialog appears with the list of available quick filters for the report.

 

 

  1. In the Quick Filters dialog, perform the following:

  1. To make the required quick filter as default, select the required quick filter, and then click Make Default.

Required Search : West Zone Quick Filter

When a quick filter is selected, Make Default option becomes available.

       

 

When the selected quick filter becomes default, then a checkmark appears under the Default section for the selected quick filter.

 

 

    • To remove default quick filter, click Remove default .
