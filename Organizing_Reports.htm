Organizing Reports

 To organize reports

1.   In the Reports section, click Options, and then click Organize.

 

      

 2. A green dotted line appears as shown in the figure below:

 

     

 

3. This enables to organize the reports from right-hand side to the folders on the left-hand side.

 

For example, in the figure below, consider a report "Group - Test". Select the report, drag and drop it on the desired folder on the left-hand side.

 

 

4. Group-Test report is now being moved to test folder  as shown below:

 

 

5. To save the changes, click Done.

6. The report Group-Test now appears under test folder.
