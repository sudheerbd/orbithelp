Grouping Columns in a Report

To group columns in a report

 

1.      In the Run Results area, different columns of the required report appear.

2.      To group by a particular column, click  next to the required column, and then select Group by this field (highlighted as “1”).

 

                                                              

 

3.      The data in the column appears grouped by the selected field.

 

                                                    

 

4.     To remove the group option on a selected column, click  next to the required column, and then remove the checkmark (highlighted as “2”) before Show in groups.
