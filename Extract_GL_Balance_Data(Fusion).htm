Extract GL Balance Data(Fusion)

This job primarily extracts all the GL Balance data from Fusion instance.

To Extract GL Setup Data from Fusion

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to , and then click Jobs.

  2. Jobs page appears with a list of Seeded and Custom jobs.

  3. Under Jobs sections, on Extract GL Balance Data (Fusion), click , and then click Schedule.

  4. Schedule Job dialog appears.

  1. In the Schedule Job dialog, on Parameters tab, perform the following:

    • In the Finance Cubes box, select the required finance cube.

    • In the Ledgers box, select the required ledger from the list of ledgers.

    • In the Start Date box, Select the required year from the list.

    • Incremental Load: this loads only the latest changed data, instead of loading the entire load from the beginning.

    • Truncate Cube Dimensions removes the existing tables in the cube.

  1. To schedule the job, click Submit.

  2. To cancel the job, click Cancel.
