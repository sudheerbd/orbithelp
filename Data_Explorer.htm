Data Explorer

To View the Data

 

1. Under Reports section, select the required report.

 Example: Required Report: Data Report

2. Select Business Object (highlighted as “1”) appear.            

Note:  Select Business Object section appears opened, by default.

3. Under the Select Business Object, the following option appears:

 

  • Search

Note: By default, Search option is in disable model.

 

 

  • On the Select Business Object tab, perform the following:

a. To select the business object, click next to Select Business Object .

b. Search Business Object dialog appears with a list of available business objects.

c. To view the corresponding domains of the business objects, move the cursor over   Business Object, click, point to Columns, and then click Domain. 

d. The domains of the respective business objects appear (highlighted as "2").

e. Select the required business object, and then click Ok.

    (or)

    Double-click the required business object.

f. The categories and the hierarchies under the selected business object (highlighted as "3") appear under the Data.

 

 

g. To search business objects/ dimensions/measures/column, enter the search term in the Search box.

h. To sort the business object/columns, click icon (highlighted as "4")

i. Under icon next to Search text box, the following options appears (highlighted as "5"):

      • Refresh

      • Sort by Ascending

      • Sort by Descending

      • Change View

      • Collapse all

      • Expand all

j. To refresh the business object, click Refresh.

k. To sort the columns in ascending order, click Sort by Ascending.

      • The categories/hierarchies or the columns under them appear in the ascending order.

l. To sort the columns in descending order, click Sort by Descending.

      • The categories/hierarchies or the columns under them appear in the descending order as shown below:   

m. To change the view under Business Objects area, click Change view  

n.  Change View provides the following two options:

      • View by category

      • View by field type

 

To add the columns under the required BO to local filters

 

a. Right-click the required column under the selected business object, and then click Add to Local Filters (highlighted as “6”).

   

b. Selected column appears in the Local Filters section.

 

 

To add the columns under the required BO to the user filters

 

a. Right-click the required column under the selected business object, and then click Add to User Filters (highlighted as “7”).

             

b. Selected column appears in the User Filters section.
