Deleting Authority

To delete authority

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to  User Management, and then click  Authorities.

  2. Authorities list appears as shown below:

 

  1. To delete an authority, click icon (highlighted as “1”) under Actions, and then click  Delete (highlighted as “2”).

 

 

  1. A message “Are you sure you want to delete?” appears.

  2. To delete the authorities, click Yes.

  3. To close the dialog box, click No.
