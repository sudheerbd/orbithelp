Opening the Chat

 

To open a chat for a selected thread

 

1. In the ORBIT application, at the top of the page, click  Collaboration Threads.

2. A window appears with a list of collaboration threads.

3. From the list of threads, click the required thread (highlighted as “1”).

     

4. The chat window for the selected thread appears as shown below:

     

5. To send a message, compose your message in the textbox at the bottom of the chat window, and then click .

     

     

6. The message appears in the chat window.

7. To Refresh the thread chat, click  Refresh.
