Creating New Hierarchy

To Create a New Hierarchy

 

 Here, the New Hierarchy  is created in Datasets. Similarly, New Hierarchy can  be created in domain as well.

 

1.      Under the Dataset Hierarchy , Business Objects folder appears. 

2.      In the Business Objects folder, right-click the required business object that needs to be opened, and then click Open.

 

            

 

3.      Two sectionsâ€” Logical Tables and Business Objects appear.

4.      The required business object and the category (highlighted as "1") appear on the extreme right-hand side of the workspace area.

 

     

                                      

5. To create a new hierarchy, right-click the required business object and then click New Hierarchy.

 

          

 

6. New Hierarchy folder appears as shown below:

  

     

 

7. Provide a suitable name for the new hierarchy as shown below:

 

       

 

8. Move the columns from the Logical Tables section to the newly created hierarchy folder under Business Objects section.

  #  Example: Newly Created Hierarchy Folder : Superstore Hierarchy

9. To save the category folder, click Save.

10. The hierarchy folder of a business object appears in the Business Objects area in the Reports (highlighted as "2"),when that specific business object is selected.   

 

     

11. Drag and drop the columns into the report configurator area.

12. Under the X-axis, drag and drop the required hierarchy from the business objects area.
