Searching a Business Object

To search a business object

 

1.      Under the Domain Hierarchy, Business Objects folder appears. 

2.      In the Business Objects folder, right-click the required business object that needs to be opened, and then click Open.

 

      

                

3.      Two sections— Logical Tables and Business Objects appear.

4.      The required business object appears on the extreme right-hand side of the workspace area.  

 

                                        

 

5.      To search a business object, click Search (highlighted as “2”).

6.      A Search box appears under the Business Objects area.

7.      In the Search box, type the search term (highlighted as “3”).

8.      Search results (highlighted as “4”) appear under the Business Object area.
