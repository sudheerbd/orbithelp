Searching Processes

To search process with Process Id

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to   Monitoring, and then click Processes.

  2. Processes page appears with a list of processes.

  3. To search a process with process id, enter the required process id in Process Id Search box.

  4. Search results appear as shown below:

  5. To search the process with advance option, click Show search options next to Process Id Search box.

  6. A list of options available as shown below:

  7. Enter the required fields and then click Search.

  8. To reset the entered search options, click Reset.
