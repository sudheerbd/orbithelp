Renaming a Business Object

To rename a business object

1.      Under the Domain Hierarchy, Business Objects folder appears. 

2.      In the Business Objects folder, right-click the required business object, and then click Rename.

        

        

                 

3.      Rename- <Business Object Name> dialog box appears.

     

      

                       

 

4.      In Rename - <Business Object Name> dialog box, perform the following:

a.       In the Name box, type a new name for the business object.

b.      In the Description box, type the description.

c.       To save the changes, click Save.

d.      To cancel the changes, click Cancel.
