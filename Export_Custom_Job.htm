Exporting a Job

To export custom job

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to , and then click Jobs.

  2. Jobs section appears with a list of available jobs.

  3. To export the required job, click  under Actions and then click Export.

 

Export option is available for Custom Jobs.

  1. Export job dialog opens.

 

  1. To submit the export job process, click Submit.

  1. The submitted process appears under the list of available processes.
