Sizing the Columns Automatically

 

To size the columns automatically

 

  1. Navigate to Reports module.

  2. To select the required folder, select My Folders.

  3. A list of folder appears on the right-hand side of the Reports tab.

  4. List of reports appears on the right-hand side of the selected folder (highlighted as "1").

  5. To open required report, right-click and then click Open (highlighted as "2").

 

  1. On the top right-hand corner of the run results area, click Options, and then click Auto Column Size (highlighted as “3”).
