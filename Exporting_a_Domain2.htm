Exporting a Domain

To export a domain

  1. In the Domain & Data Models section, under Actions, click .

  2. To export a domain, click Export.

  3. Export Domain dialog box appears.

  4. In the Export Domain dialog box, Run Immediately check box appears selected, by default.

  5. To export the domain immediately, click Submit.

  6. To schedule the domain export at the required time, clear the Run Immediately check box.

  7. The Schedule tab becomes available.

  8. Schedule the domain export at the required time, and then click Submit.

  9. The request gets submitted as a process under Monitoring->Processes.

 

To export a domain from Domain Hierarchy

  1. Beside the Domain Hierarchy area, Semantic Layers area appears.

  2. In the Semantic Layers area, the domain menu appears.

  3. To export the domain, click Export in the domain menu.

  4. Export Domain dialog box appears.

  5. To export the domain, click Submit.

  6. The exported domain appears as a process in the Processes section under Scheduled Processes.
