Working with Pivot

 

Pivot tables provide the ability to obtain different perspectives of the same data. Here, pivot option is available in View Mode.

To work with pivot

1.      In the Reports area, right click the required report, and then click Run.

2.       On the reports menu above the Run Result area, click Pivot.

 

                                                      

 

3.       Pivot area appears.

4.      The selected fields from Business Objects area (highlighted as “2”) appear under All fields.  

5.      Under Values area, drag and drop the measures from All fields area (highlighted as “3”).

6.      Under Row Labels area, drag and drop the dimensions from All fields area (highlighted as “4”).

7.      Under Column Labels area, drag and drop the dimensions from All fields area (highlighted as “5”).

 

       

                                                         

 

8.      The pivot table (highlighted as “6”) appears.

9.      To view the chart for the selected pivot table, click Chart.

10.  The chart (highlighted as “7”) appears as shown below:

 

        

                                                                         

 

¥ Row Labels represents the X-axis and Column Labels represents the Y-axis.

 

11.  To view different types of charts, click  on the Chart option.

12.  The following chart options appear:

a.       Column

b.      Line

c.       Area
