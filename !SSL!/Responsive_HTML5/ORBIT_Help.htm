Orbit Help

Orbit Online Help explains how to use the application. It is designed for the administrators/business professionals/end users to understand the workflow of the ORBIT Analytics Application. The end users might want to work with the application while reading this online help. 

 

Preface

This section deals with intended audience, about online help,conventions, and feedback.  

Chapter 1

This chapter deals with the product overview,application architecture, and data model of Orbit.

Chapter 2

This chapter deals with the user interface. You will understand how to get started with Orbit application, log onto the application, access the account details, change password, configure the settings, log out of the application. You will as well get introduced to the various application features and the Home page features.

Chapter 3

This chapter introduces you to the Business Units. You will be introduced to Business Units; you will understand how to edit and filter the business units.

Chapter 4

This chapter deals with the User Management feature of the application. You will understand how to work with Users, Groups, Roles, Authorities, and Permissions.

Chapter 5

This chapter deals with the Business Models.  You will understand how to work with Domains, Data sources, Security and Meta Objects.

Chapter 6

This chapter deals with Monitoring.  You will understand how to work with processes and report statistics.

Chapter 7

This Chapter deals with Settings. You will understand how to work with Diagnostics, Preferences, and Locales.

Chapter 8

This chapter deals with Reports. You will understand about the menu options in the reports, business objects area, report configurator area, run results area, properties, performing various options on reports, and the calculation column.

Chapter 9

This chapter deals with Data Reports. You will understand about various features and operations of Data Reports.

Chapter 10

This chapter deals with Analysis. You will understand about various features and operations of Analysis.

Chapter 11

This chapter deals with Dashboards. You will understand about various features and operations of Dashboards.

Chapter 12

This chapter deals with Publisher Reports. You will understand about various features and operations of Publisher Reports.

Chapter 13

This chapter deals with Script Reports. You will understand about various features and operations of Script Reports.

Chapter 14

This chapter deals with Datasets. You will understand how to perform various operations on the datasets.

Chapter 15

This chapter deals with widget settings of different charts.

Chapter 16

This chapter deals with Excel Edge application.

Chapter 17

This chapter deals with Orbit extensions such as Orbit Discoverer Migration

Chapter 18

This chapter deals with some of the Practice Examples such as creating various reports using domain and creating various reports using datasets.
