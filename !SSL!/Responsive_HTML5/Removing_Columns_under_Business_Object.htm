Removing Columns under Business Object

To remove columns under a business object

 

1.      Under the Domain Hierarchy, Business Objects folder appears. 

2.      In the Business Objects folder, right-click the required business object that needs to be opened, and then click Open.

 

       

                       

 

3.      Two sections— Logical Tables and Business Objects appear.

4.      The required business object (highlighted as “1”) appears on the extreme right-hand side of the workspace area.

 

 

 

5. On the right-hand side corner beside Business Objects,   Remove selected columns or categories icon (highlighted as “2”)appears.  

                                      

                  

    

 

6.      To remove a column, select the  column under Business Objects, and then click  Remove selected columns or categories (highlighted as “2” in the above figure).
