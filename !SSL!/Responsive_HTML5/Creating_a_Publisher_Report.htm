Creating a Publisher Report

To Create a Publisher Report

 

1. In the Reports section, click   New Report, and then click Publisher Report (highlighted as “1”).

 

 

 

2. A new window appears with Select Business Object, Bands, and Band Details sections (highlighted as “2”). 

 

     

 

3. In the Select Business Object area, select the required business object.

?   Example: Required Business Object: Superstore1

 

4. The columns under the selected business object (highlighted as “3”) appear.

 

 

 

5. Under the Bands section, MainBand appears.

 

¥   One band can have multiple child bands under it and a band can also have multiple queries under it.

¥   Bands in the Publisher Report maps to Names in Excel sheet.

 

6. Under MainBand, perform the following:

 

  a. On the MainBand, right-click and then select New Band to create a new band.

 

    (OR)

 

    Hover the cursor on the MainBand, click , and then click New Band.

 

?   Example: New Band: Header

 

               

 

      b. When a specific band is selected, the band details occur under Band Details

         (highlighted as “4”).

 

 

 

 

¥   You can change the band name either by double-clicking it or you can change it under Band Details.

¥   The default orientation is Horizontal. You change the orientation to Vertical as well. The orientation describes direction of the data display.

¥   Here, Header band is for the Header band that we created in the excel sheet. Now, we would be creating data band to extract the data under Header.

 

7. On the MainBand, right-click and then select New Band to create a new band.

 

      (OR)

 

    Hover the cursor on the MainBand, click , and then click New Band.

 

?   Example: New Band: Data

 

 

8. On the Data band (highlighted as “5”), right-click, and then select New Query.

 

     (OR)

      Hover the cursor on the Data band, click , and then click New Query.

 

 

9. A new query option (highlighted as “6”) appears.

 

   

 

10. Select the query option.

 

11. Query Details (highlighted as “7”) and Query (highlighted as “8”) sections appear.

   

     

 

12. Drag and drop the columns from Business Object area to the  Configurator under Query Details.

 

 

You can also use the calculation column to create your own formula (highlighted as “9”) by clicking on New Calculation Column.

 

  

 

13. Under the Query section, a query is built based on the dragged columns.

     

 

14. Save the report under appropriate folder, by clicking Save.

 

Once the report is saved, apply the template of the required format. Here, the Excel template would be applied.
