Navigating to the Required Report

If you are in the midst of a report or you want to view another report, then perform the following procedure.

To navigate to the required report

 

1. On the top of the reports page, Reports (highlighted as “1”) menu appears. 

2. To navigate to the required report, click Reports, and then select the required report from the available reports.

3. To return to the reports home with the complete report tree, click Reports menu, and then click  Reports (highlighted as “2”).
