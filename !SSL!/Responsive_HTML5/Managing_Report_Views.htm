Managing Quick Filter

Since one report can have multiple quick filters, it is essential to manage the reports.

To Manage the Quick Filter

1. For the required report, on the top right-hand side corner, click Options, and then click Manage Quick Filter.

2. Manage Report Quick Filters dialog appears with the list of available quick filters for the report.

 

   

 

3. In the Manage Report Quick Filters dialog, perform the following:

 

    a. To make the required quick filter as default, select the required report quick filter, and then click Make as default.

 

?    Example : Required Search : West Zone Quick Filter

 

¥  Initially, Make as default option appears grayed, but when a quick filter is selected, Make as default option becomes available.

              

¥  When the selected quick filter becomes default, then a checkmark appears under the Default section for the selected quick filter.

 

 

4. To delete a search, select the required search, and then click  Delete under Actions.

5. To save the changes, click Save.
