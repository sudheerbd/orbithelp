Viewing Scheduled Processes

To view the scheduled processes

 

1.      On the left-hand side menu, click  Monitoring.

2.      A list of scheduled processes appears.

 

 

 

3.      To view the process under the currently logged in user, click My processes (highlighted as “1”).

4.      To view all the scheduled processes, click All processes.

 

 

5.      To refresh the submitted processes, click Refresh.
