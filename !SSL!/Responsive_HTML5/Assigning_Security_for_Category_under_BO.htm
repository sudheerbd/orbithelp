Assigning Security for Category under BO

 

To assign security for Category under Business Object

 

1. For the required business object,under the Business Objects section, select the required category (highlighted as "1").

 

  •  When the category is selected, Assigned Roles and Assigned Users  appear under Security.

 

    

 

2. To assign the required role to the category, click Search for items to add (highlighted as "2").

3. To assign the required user to the category, click Search for items to add (highlighted as "3").

4. Select the required roles/users from the available list of roles.

 

   

   

5. The selected roles/users appear under Assigned Roles/Assigned Users respectively.
