Jobs

To work with Job

 

1.      On the left-hand side menu, point to Business Models and then click Security.

2.      Security page appears with the following tabs:

·    Business Objects

·   Jobs

3.      To work with Job, click the Jobs tab (highlighted as “1”).

 

 

4.      A list of jobs (highlighted as “2”) appears.

5.      To assign roles for a job, perform the following:

a.    Under Jobs, select the required job, and then click Search for items to add  beside Assigned Roles.

b.   A list of available roles appears

 

 

   c.       From the roles list, select the required role.

   d.      The selected roles appear under Assigned Roles.

6.      To assign users for a job, perform the following:

a.    Under Jobs, select the required job, and then click Search for items to add  beside Assigned Users.

b.   A list of available users (highlighted as “3”) appears

 

c.       From the users list, select the required user.

d.      The selected user appears under Assigned Users.

 

7.      To save the changes, click Save (highlighted as “4”).  

8.      To refresh the jobs, click Refresh (highlighted as “5”).

 

          

 

9.      To filter the jobs, perform the following:

a.  Under Jobs, click  to filter the jobs.

b. Textboxes appear under Name and Description (highlighted as “6”).

c.       To filter the job, type the search term (highlighted as “7”) in the Name/Description field.
