Deleting a Report

 

To delete a report

1.      Under the Reports area, the required report appears.

2.      Right-click the required report, and then click Delete (highlighted as “1”).

 

       (OR)

 

      For the selected report, click  , and then click Delete.

 

             

 

  1. A confirmation message “Please confirm, you cannot undo this operation” appears.

 

    

4.      To delete the report, click Ok. 

5.      The deleted report does not appear under Reports area.
