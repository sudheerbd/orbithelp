Scheduling a Report

To schedule a Report

1.      Under the Reports area, the required reports appear.

2.      Right-click the required report, and then click Schedule.

 

(OR)

            For the selected report, click  , and then click Schedule.

 

      

               

 

3.      Schedule Report -<Report Name> dialog box appears.

4.      Schedule section provides the following section:

a.      Parameters. See Parameters  (highlighted as “1”)

b.      Schedule  See Schedule (highlighted as “2”)

c.       Output & Delivery See Output & Delivery (highlighted as “3”)

d.      Notifications See Notifications (highlighted as “4”)

 

                            Figure: Displaying Schedule Report- <Report Name>

 

5.      To schedule the report, fill the required fields in the above mentioned tabs, and then click Submit. See Monitoring
