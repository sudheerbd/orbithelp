Introduction to ORBIT Analytics Features

 

ORBIT Analytics application comprises of the following features:

 

  • Home

          Home section contains the following:

o  Help

o Downloads

o  Lock Screen

o   Excel Edge

o  Favorites

o  Recent

o  Unlock home widgets

 

  • Reports

Reports section displays five types of reports

o   Data Reports

o   Analysis

o   Dashboards

o Publisher Reports

o Script Reports

 

 

  • Datasets

Datasets contain Domains. Domains consist of the following:

    • Physical Model

    • Logical Model

    • Business Objects

     

  • Business Models

Business Models consists of the following:

o   Domains

o   Data Sources

o   Security

o   Meta Objects

  • User Management

User Management section consists of the following:

    • Users

    • Groups

    • Roles

    • Authorities

    • Permissions

  • Monitoring

Monitoring section consists of the following:

 

    • Processes

    • Report Statistics

 

  • Settings

Settings section consists of the following:

    • Diagnostics

    • Preferences

    • Locales

 

  • Business Units

Business Units section enables to  create new business units and perform various actions on Business Units.
