Working with Drilldowns

To work with drilldowns

 

1.      In the Measures (Y-Axis) area (highlighted as “1”), drag and drop the required column.

 

            Example: Required Column: SUM (Sales)

 

          

        

       2.      In the Dimensions(X-Axis) area (highlighted as “2”), drag and drop the required columns.

      

                    Example: Required Columns: Region

                                                                State

 

 

                     

 

3.      To view the drilldown of the report, click Enable hierarchy  icon next to Category (X-Axis).

4.      The fields under Category (X-Axis), appear as shown below (highlighted as “3”):

 

                       

             5.      Disable hierarchy button (highlighted as “4”) appears next to DIMENSIONS (X-Axis).

 

¥  When Disable hierarchy button appears, it implies that Drilldown is applied on the columns.

 

6.      On the Run Results area, in the displayed report, click on the data to move to the next hierarchy.

 

                  For Example: Here, the report displayed below shows the sales by regions – Central, East, West, and Mountain. When West region is selected, the report appears as in <Sales by States in the West region> figure.
