Assign Roles & Users

Under Assign Roles & Users sub-tab, the user can:

·         Assign required roles to a specific business object

·         Assign required user to a specific business object 

 

Assigning Roles to Business Object

To assign roles to Business Object

  1. On the Assign Roles & Users tab, under Business Objects area, the available business objects appear.

  2. To assign the role for the selected business object (highlighted as “1”) perform the following:

a.  On the right-hand side, Assigned Roles (highlighted as “2”) area appears.

b.  To assign a new role to the selected business object, click  Search for items to add (highlighted as “3”).

 

 

c.  A list of available roles appears.

d.  From the available roles, select the required role (highlighted as “4”).

e.  The selected role appears under Assigned Roles (highlighted as “5”).
