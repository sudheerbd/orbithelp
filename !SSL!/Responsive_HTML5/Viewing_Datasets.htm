Viewing Datasets

To view a dataset

1.      On the Left-hand side, point to, and then click Datasets.

 

        

               

2.      Datasets page appears.

3.      In the Datasets area, a list of available datasets appears.

4.       The newly created dataset (highlighted as “1”) appears in this datasets list.

 

                                      

5.      To refresh the list of datasets, click Refresh (highlighted as “2”).

6.      To filter the datasets, click  (highlighted as “3”).
