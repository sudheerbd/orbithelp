Filtering Business Units

To filter business objects

1.      In the Business Units section, click .

2.      Textboxes to filter the business units (highlighted as “1”) appear under Name, Name Key, Admin Email, Admin Username, and Description.

 

 

3.      Under the required column, type the required value to filter.

    • Example: Required Column: Name

    • Example: Required Value:  OR

4.      Based on the filter value, the search results appear.

 

 

5.      To refresh the business units, click Refresh .
