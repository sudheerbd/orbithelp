Editing the Business Unit

 

To edit the business unit

  1. On the menu bar, click Business Units.

  2. Business Units page appears.

  3. In the Business Units page, the existing business units appear.

  4. For the required business unit, click Edit.

  5. Edit Business Unit - <Name > dialog box appears.

        

                                             

 

  1. Edit the required fields, and then click Save.

 

  • You cannot edit the fields: Admin Username

  • Name key which is used to generate the links for activation emails is not allowing (-) Hyphen whereas (-) Hyphen valid is a symbol for a host name as long as it is not in the first or last position.

  • Description, Banner Text, and Copyright Text fields are optional.

  • You can add a logo by clicking Browse.
