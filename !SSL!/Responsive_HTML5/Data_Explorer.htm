Data Explorer

To View the Data Explorer

 

1. On the Reports section, click New Report, and then click the required report.

 

                   Example: Required Report: Data Report

 

            2. Data Explorer (highlighted as “1”) and Widgets (highlighted as “2”) tabs appear.

 

 

¥    Data Explorer section appears open, by default.

 

3. Under the Data Explorer tab, perform the following:

a. On the Select Business Object area, click Choose business object .

b. Search Business Object dialog appears with a list of available business objects.

c. To view the corresponding domains of the business objects, move the cursor over Business Object, click , point to Columns, and then click Domain.  

d. The domains of the respective business objects (highlighted as "3") appear as shown below:

   

             

 

e. Select the required business object, and then click Ok.

    (or)

    Double-click the required business object.

f. The selected business object (highlighted as “4”) appears under the Data Explorer.

 

 

g. To search business objects/ dimensions/measures, click Search, and then type the search term in the search box.

h. To upload a dataset file, click Upload File.

i. To create joins between the tables, click Relationships.

j. To change the view under Business Objects area, click Change view  

k. Change View provides the following two options:

o   View by category

o   View by field type

 

                To add the columns under the required BO to the data filters

 

                   a. Right-click the required column under the selected business object, and then click Add to Data Filters (highlighted as “1”).

   

                          

                                        

 

                       b. The selected column appears in the Data Filters section.

 

 

                To add the columns under the required BO to the user filters

 

                   a. Right-click the required column under the selected business object, and then click Add to User Filters (highlighted as “1”).

                                        

                             

 

                       b. The selected column appears in the User Filters section.

 

4. Under the Widgets tab, perform the following:

a. To search business objects/ dimensions/measures, click Search, and then type the search term in the search box.

b. Change View provides the following two options:

o   View by category

o   View by field type

                 5. The following options are common to both Data Explorer and Widgets:

a. To collapse the complete structure under business object area, click Collapse all.

            b.  To expand the complete structure under business object area, click Expand all.

c. To refresh the structure under business object, click Refresh .
