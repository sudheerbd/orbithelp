Organizing Reports

 To organize reports

1.   Under Reports area, click Organize reports.   

       

     

 

2.      Organize Reports dialog box appears.

 

    

 

      3.   In Organize Reports dialog box, under Reports, in My Folders, the available folders with reports under it appear.

4.      Drag the required report that needs to be moved from source folder and drop it into the destination folder.

?        Example:  Source Folder: Superstore Folder

?                           Destination Folder: Test    

?                           Required report: Sample Report-Superstore

 

 

5.      The report (highlighted as “1”) appears under the destination folder 

6.      To save the changes, click Save.

7.      To close the dialog box, click Cancel.
