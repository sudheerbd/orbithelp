Editing Authority

To edit authority

1.      On the User Management menu, click Authorities .

2.      Authorities area appears with a list of authorities.

3.      For the required authority, click  Edit.

 

      • Example: Required Authority: XREPORT_IORB3

                                                               

4.      Edit Authority <authority name> dialog box appears.

      

      

     

                                    

5.      Edit the required fields, and then click Save to make the changes.

6.      To close the dialog box, click Cancel.
