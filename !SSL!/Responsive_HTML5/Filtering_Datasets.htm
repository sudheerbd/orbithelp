Filtering Datasets

 

To filter the datasets

1.      Under Datasets, click.

2.      Textboxes to filter the datasets appear under Name, Description, and Created By fields (highlighted as “1”).

 

                                           

3.      Under the required field, type the required term to filter the values under the field.

4.      Based on the filter term (highlighted as “2”), the search results (highlighted as “3”) appear as shown:
