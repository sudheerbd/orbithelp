Logging into Excel Edge

          To Logon to Excel Edge

              1.   On the ORBIT Analytics menu, click Login.  

              2. A message box opens.

              3. Follow the instructions mentioned in the message box as shown below:

                 

 

           4. The excel sheet with above-mentioned steps appear as follows:

              

         5. To log onto Excel Edge application, click Login (highlighted as “1”).

         6. A section to login to Excel Edge appears on the right-hand side of the Excel Edge (highlighted as “2”).

              

 

7. In the login section, perform the following:

    a. In the Username box, type the required username.

    b.  In the Password box, type the required password.

    c.  To logon to the application, click Login.
