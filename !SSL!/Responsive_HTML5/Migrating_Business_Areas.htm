Migrating Business Areas

To Migrate Business Areas

 

1.      In the address bar of the browser, type the ORBIT Migration Utilities URL.

2.      ORBIT Migration Utilities page appears.

3.      On the left-hand side, the menu appears as shown below:

 

 

4.      From the left-hand side menu, click ORBIT Discoverer, and then click Business Area.

5.      Discoverer Migration page appears with a list of available business areas.

 

 

6.      In the ORBIT-Oracle Discoverer Migration, under SELECT BUSINESS AREAS section, select the required option (highlighted as “2”), and then click Download Schedule XML (highlighted as “1”).

 

 

7.      The selected business area, gets downloaded as an xml file (highlighted as “3”).
