Viewing Migrated Reports

To view the migrated reports

 

1.      On the left-hand side, click Reports.

 

 

2.      Reports page appears with a list of available reports.

3.      In Reports, under My Folders, Discoverer Reports folder (highlighted as “1”) appears.

 

     

   

 

4.      Under Discoverer Reports folder, the migrated reports appear (highlighted as “2”).

5.      To run the required report, right-click the required report, and then click Open.

6.      The columns under the required report appear in the run results area.

7.      To view the values under the columns in the required report, click Run above run results area.

8.      The required report appears with the values.
