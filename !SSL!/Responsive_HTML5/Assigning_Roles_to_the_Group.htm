Assigning Roles to the Group

 

To assign a Role to the Group

  1. Select the required group, and then click  next to Assigned Roles.

  2. A list of roles appears.

 

        

                                                

3.      Select the required role from the list.

    • Example: Required Role: Admin

  1. Under Assigned Roles, the selected role appears.

 

               

                                                   

  1. To save the changes, click Save.
