Adding a Report to Favorites

To add a report to favorites

1.      Under the Reports area, the required report appears.

2.      Right-click the required report, and then click Add to favorites.  

 

(OR)

      For the selected report, click  , and then click Add to favorites. 

 

       

               

3.      A success message appears.

(OR)

To add a report to favorites

1.      Under the Reports area, click Organize reports  icon  

 

     

 

 

2.      Organize Reports dialog box appears with two sections – Reports (highlighted as “1”) and Favorites (highlighted as “2”).

                     

 

 

3.      Under Reports, from the available folders, drag the required report and drop it in the Favorites section.

4.      To save the changes, click Save.

5.      To close the dialog box, click Cancel.
