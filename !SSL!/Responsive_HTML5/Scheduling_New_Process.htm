Scheduling New Process

To schedule a new process

1.      On the menu bar, click Monitoring.

2.      Scheduled Processes page appears with a list of processes.   

3.      On the right-hand side of the window, click Schedule New Process (highlighted as “1”).

 

                                       Figure: Displaying Schedule New Process

 

4.      A dialog box with the following four tabs appear:

·         Parameters

·         Schedule

·         Output & Delivery

·         Notifications

 

          

                                                 Figure: New Schedule Dialog Box
