Configuring List of Values

To Configure List of Values

 

1.      Under the Domain Hierarchy, right-click the required business object, and then click Open.

 

       

                                          

 

 

2.      Two sections— Logical Tables<Business Object Name> and Business Objects— appear on the right-hand side.

3.      Under the Logical Tables sections, right-click the required column, and then click List of values.

 

                                        

 

4.      List of values dialog box appears.

 

                                        

 

5.      In the Select Logical Model list, click the required logical model.

6.      In the Logical Tables area, the tables/views/synonyms under the selected business object appear.

7.      From the available columns, drag and drop the required columns in Value Field, Display Field(s), and Filter(s).

 

                                        

 

8.      To save the list of values, click Ok.

9.      To close the dialog box, click Cancel.
