Label filters

Label filters filter by group labels. Label filters are available to Row labels and Column labels. Label filters contain the following filters:

·         Equals

·         Does not equal

·         Begins with

·         Does not begin with

·         Ends with

·         Does not end with

·         Contains

·         Does not contain

·         Greater than

·         Greater than or equal to

·         Less than

·         Less than or equal to

·         Between

·         Not Between

 

                            

 

 To set the label filters

 

1.      Under Row labels/Column labels, click the required field, and then point to Label filters, and then click the required filter value.

 

                                           

 

2.      Label filter <field name> dialog box appears.

 

        

                    

 

3.      In the filters list, click the required option.

4.      In the textbox next to the filter list, type the filter value.

5.      Select the Case sensitive checkbox, to make the applied filter case sensitive.

 

       

      

6.      To save the changes, click OK.

7.      To close the dialog box, click Cancel.
