Configuring Widget Settings

To Configure Widget Settings

 

1.      In the Report Configurator, click Widget Settings tab (highlighted as “1”) to configure the widget setting for the required report.

2.      The following tabs appear:

·         General (highlighted as “2”)

·         Paging (highlighted as “3”)

 

     

 

3.      Under General tab, perform the following:

 

a.      In the Row Number box, type/select the required option.

b.       In the Column Lines box, type/select the required option.

c.      In the Row Lines box, type/select the required option.

d.       In the Row Banding box, type/select the required option.

e.       In the Locked Column box, type/select the required option.

f.      To save the changes, click Apply.

 

4.      Under Paging tab, perform the following:

a.        In the Paging box, type/select the required option.

b.      In the Position box, type or select the required option from the following:

                                                              i.      top

                                                           iii.    bottom

 

c.       In the Size box, type/select the required number.

d.      To save the changes, click Apply.
