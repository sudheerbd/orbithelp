Sharing a Report

Sharing a report can be done in the two ways.

 

Procedure 1

1.      Under Reports area, click Share reports.

2.      Share Reports dialog appears with the following three tabs:

a.       Assigned Roles & Users

b.      Assign to Role

c.       Assign to User

 

Assigned Roles & Users

 

1.  Under the Assigned Roles & Users tab, the following three sections:

a.       Reports (highlighted as “1”)

b.      Assigned Roles (highlighted as “2”)

c.       Assigned Users (highlighted as “3”)

 

        

 

2. From the Reports section, select the required folder, sub folder or the individual report.

        

          Example: Required Report: Charts

 

  • You can either assign a role or a user or both for the selected folder/report.

3. To assign the role, click Search for items to add.

4. From the available roles, select the required role.

        Example: Assigned Role: Analyst

5. The selected role appears under the Assigned Roles section.

 

6. To assign the user, click Search for items to add.

7. From the available users, select the required users.

 

  Example: Assigned User: XXFORX

 

8. The selected user appears under the Assigned Users section.

 

  • For example, considering the above scenario, if the CHARTS report is shared to ANALYST role, and XXFORX user, then the report will be available to all the users having the “Analyst” role irrespective of the user and the report will also be available to “XXFORX” user.

 

9. The Assigned Roles/Assigned Users can Run, Edit, Delete, Copy, Schedule, Export, and Generate Link.

 

  • Run and Schedule options are available to the Assigned Roles/Assigned Users, by default.

 

10. To select the other options (Edit Delete, Copy, Export and Generate Link), select the checkbox below the required options below.  

 

 

    

 

 

11. To share the folders to the subfolders of a main folder, select the Apply to subfolder checkbox.

12. To save the changes, click Save.

 

Assign to Role

 

1. Under the Assign to Role tab, the following two sections appear:

a.       Roles (highlighted as “1”)

b.      Assign Reports (highlighted as “2”)

 

  

 

2. Under Roles, the available roles appear.

3. Under Assign Reports, the available reports appear.

4. Under Roles, select the required role.

5. The reports under the specific role appears under the Assign Reports section.

6. Select the required report, then perform the required actions((Run, Edit, Delete, Copy, Schedule, Export and Generate Link).

7. To save the changes, click Save.

 

 

 

8. To show only the shared reports, click the Show Shared Reports Only checkbox (highlighted as “3”) on Assign Reports section.

9. To show all the reports, deselect the Show All check box (highlighted as “3”) on the Assign Reports section.

10. To search the reports, click Search.  

 

Assign to User

 

1. Under the Assign to User tab, the following two sections appear:

a.       Users (highlighted as “1”)

b.      Assign Reports (highlighted as “2”)

   

 

2. Under Users, the available users appear.

3. Under Assign Reports, the available reports appear.

4. Under Users, select the required user.

5. The reports under the specific user appears under the Assign Reports section.

6. Select the required report, then perform the required actions((Run, Edit, Delete, Copy, Schedule, Export and Generate Link).

7. To save the changes, click Save.

 

 

8. To show only the shared reports, click the Show Shared Reports Only checkbox (highlighted as “3”) on Assign Reports section.

9. To show all the reports, deselect the Show All check box (highlighted as “3”) on the Assign Reports section.

10. To search the reports, click Search.  

 

 

Procedure 2

 

1.      Under the Reports area, the required report appears.

                Example: Required Report: User Filters Test

 

2.      Right-click the required report, and then click Share.

(OR)

            For the selected report, click , and then click Share.

 

 

           

 

3.      Share<report name> dialog box appears.

4.      Sharing Reports is done in the following two ways:

a.  On Assigned Roles, click to add a role.

b . A list of available roles appears.

c. Select the required role.

d. The required role appears under Assigned Roles.

 

This shares the report to the required assigned roles (highlighted as “1”).

                         Example: Required Role: USER

 

(OR)

 

a.  On Assigned Users, click to add a user.

b . A list of available users appears.

c. Select the required user.

d. The required user appears under Assigned Users.  

.     

 This shares the report to the required users (highlighted as “2”).  

                        Example: Required User: XXFORX

 

             

 

5. To delete the assigned user/role, select the required user/role and then click  Delete User/Delete Role respectively.

6. To save the changes, click Ok.

 

  • When the report is shared in the Folder View, then the same sharing permissions appear when the report is viewed under View by widget, Favorites and Recent sections.
