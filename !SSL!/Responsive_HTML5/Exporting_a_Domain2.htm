Exporting a Domain

To export a domain

1.      Beside the Domain Hierarchy area, Semantic Layers area appears.

2.    In the Semantic Layers area, the domain menu appears.

3.   To export the domain, click Export in the domain menu.

 

 

4.  Export Domain dialog appears.

 

 

5.   To export the domain, click Submit.
