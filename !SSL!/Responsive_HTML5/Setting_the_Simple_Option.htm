Setting the Simple Option

To set the simple option

1.      On the Simple tab, under the Recurrence area (highlighted as “1”), select any one of the following:

 

 

                                           Figure: Displaying Simple Tab

 

·         Never Repeat

·         Repeat

 

  •  When Repeat is selected, two other options— Every and Months— appear

 

2.      In the Every box, type/select the required number.

3.      In the Months box, type/select the required months.

 

      

                                                      Figure: Displaying Simple Tab
