Importing Domain

To Import a Domain

1. Login to the target ORBIT instance, where the domain needs to be imported.

2. On the left-hand side menu, point to  Monitoring, and then click Processes.

3. In the Processes page, on the top right-hand side, click New Process, and then click Import Domain/Report.

4. Import Domain/Report dialog appears.

5. In the Import Domain/Report dialog, on the Parameters tab, click Upload to upload the required xml file of domain that must be imported, and then click Submit.

 

       

 

6. A success message appears once the domain is imported, and a new process is created under Processes under Monitoring.
