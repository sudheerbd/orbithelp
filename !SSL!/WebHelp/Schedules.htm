Schedules

Schedules option shows all the scheduled processes of all the reports available to the user.

To view scheduled processes of all the reports:

  1. On the Reports page, in the Reports menu, click Options and then click Schedules.

  2. Processes window appears with a list of scheduled processes available to the user.

  3. Select a process to view its Schedule Information and Parameters.

  4. On the right-hand side, under Schedule Information section, Details, Start Date, and Next Run, Phase, and Status details appears as shown above.

  5. The above process is a recurring schedule process. Hence, that time stamp appears in the Next Run under Schedule Information.
