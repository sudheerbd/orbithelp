Submitting the Process

To submit the process

 

  1. In the ORBIT XLEdge application, in the Submit/Schedule tab, click Expand panel (highlighted as "1"). 

           

 

  1. Submit/Schedule panel expands.

  2. To submit the process immediately, select Run Immediately checkbox (highlighted as “2”).

  3. To notify the user about the end of the process, select Notify me checkbox.

  4. To submit the request click Submit.       

  1. A success message appears.

  2. To view the submitted report, click Back to Reports (highlighted as “3”) on the Parameters tab.

 

   

  1. This displays the Scheduled Process tab.

 

 

 

  1. On the Scheduled Process tab, click Expand panel (highlighted as “4”), to view the submitted process.

 

 

  1. To refresh the list of scheduled processes, click Refresh.
