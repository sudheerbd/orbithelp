Copying a Domain

To copy a Domain

 

  1. In Domain & Data Models section, under Actions, click ..

  2. To copy a domain, click Copy.

 

 

  1. Copy- <Domain Name> dialog appears.

  2. In the Copy - <Domain Name>, perform the following:

    1. In the Name box, by default copy domain name appears.

Copy of Sample doc

  1. In the Description box, type the required description.

 

  1. To save the changes, click Save.

  2. To close the dialog, click Cancel or .
