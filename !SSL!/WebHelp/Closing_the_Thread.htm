Closing the Thread

To close a thread

1. In the Threads section, under Actions, click   against the thread you want to close, and then click Close.

2.Status Change dialog box appears.

3. To close the thread, click Yes in Status Change dialog box.

 

Note: A Collaboration thread status change request processed notification appears briefly at the top of the ORBIT application window.

 

4. To cancel close thread request, click No.
