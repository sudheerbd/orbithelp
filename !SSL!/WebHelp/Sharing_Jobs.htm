Sharing a Job

To Share a Job

 

  1. Under Jobs section, for the required job, click   under Actions, and then click Share.

  1. Share - <Job Name > dialog appears.

 

  1. Under Share-<Job Name> dialog, the following can be performed:

    • Assigning Jobs to Roles
    • Assigning Jobs to Users

 

Sharing Jobs to Roles

To Assign Jobs to Roles

  1. To assign the required roles for a job, click   for Assigned Roles.

  2. The available roles appear.

  3. To search a role, type the required search term in the Search box.

  4. From the search results, select the required role as shown below:

 

  1. The selected role appears under Assigned Roles.

This implies that the selected job is shared to all the users who have the roles: ORBIT ADMIN ROLE and ORBIT SUPER USER ROLE.

 

Sharing Jobs to Users

To Share the Jobs to Users

  1. To assign the required users for a job, click for Assigned Users.

  2. The available users appear.

  3. To search a user, type the required search term in the Search box.

  4. From the search results select the required role as shown below:

 

 

 

  1. The selected users appear under Assigned Users.

This implies that the required job is shared to the selected users irrespective of the roles assigned to that particular user.

  1. To save the changes, click Ok.

  1. To close the dialog without saving the changes, click Close / .
