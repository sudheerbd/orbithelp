Assigning Groups to the Specific User

To assign groups to the specific user

 

  1. Select the required user, and then click  next to Assigned Groups.

  2. A list of groups (highlighted as "1") appears.

 

         

                                                        

  1. Select the required group from the list.

  2. Under Assigned Groups, the selected group appears.

 

        

      5. To save the changes, click Save.
