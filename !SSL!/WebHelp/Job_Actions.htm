Job Actions

To view actions of a Jobs, perform the following:

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to , and then click Jobs.

  2. Jobs window appears with a list of available jobs.

  3. In the Jobs section, under Actions, click  (highlighted as "1").

  4. The following options appear:

    • Scheduling a Job

    • Editing a Job

    • Pause a Job

    • Resume a Job

    • Exporting a Job

    • Removing a Job

    • Sharing a Job
