Working with Nodes

To work with nodes

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to  Monitoring, and then click  Nodes.

  2. Nodes page appears.

  3. In the Nodes page, under Nodes section, the configured nodes appear.

 

  1. To download the log for the required node, click  Download Log.

  1. The file appears with .log extension.

 

 

 

  1. To open the log file, click the downloaded log file. 

  2. The log file opens in the text file format.

  3. To refresh the nodes, click  Refresh.

  4. To sort in ascending order, hover the cursor over the required field, click  , and then click Sort Ascending.

  5. To sort in descending order, hover the cursor over the required field, click ,and then click Sort Descending.

 

Ascending/Descending order can be applied on Node Name, Status, and Log File.
