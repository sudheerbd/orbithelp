Viewing Datasets

To view a dataset

  1. On the Left-hand side, point to, and then click Datasets.

  1. Datasets page appears.

  2. In the Datasets area, a list of available datasets appears.

  3. The newly created dataset (highlighted as “1”) appears in this datasets list.

 

                                      

  1. To refresh the list of datasets, click Refresh (highlighted as “2”).
