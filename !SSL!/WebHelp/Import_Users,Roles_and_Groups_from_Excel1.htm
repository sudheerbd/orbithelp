Import Users,Roles and Groups from Excel

To Import Users, Roles, and Groups from Excel

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to , and then click Jobs.

  2. Jobs page appears with a list of Seeded and Custom jobs.

  3. Under Jobs sections, on Import Users, Roles and Groups from Excel, click , and then click Schedule.

  4. Import Users, Roles and Groups from Excel dialog appears.     

           

         

    

  1. Under the Parameters section, click Download Sample Excel.      

  1. To upload the excel file, click Upload (highlighted as "1")

  2. The users can download the Excel worksheet  and fill in the details of the following in the Excel worksheet:

 

      • users (highlighted as "2")

      • roles (highlighted as "3")

      • groups (highlighted as "4")

      • user-group_mapping (highlighted as "5")

      • user-role_mapping (highlighted as "6")

      • group-role_mapping (highlighted as "7")
