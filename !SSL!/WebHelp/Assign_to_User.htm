Assign to User

This feature assigns the business objects to the available user.

 

To assign BO to User  

1.      Under the Assign to User tab, the available users and their assigned business objects appear.

2.      To view the assigned user for a business object, select the required user.

3.      For the selected user, its assigned business object appears under Assigned Business Objects.

4.   To assign a business object for a specific user (highlighted as “1”), click  Search for items to add.

5.      A list of  available business objects appear.

6.   For the selected user, select the required business object (highlighted as “2”) from the list, and then click save.
