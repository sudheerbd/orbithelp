Collaboration

Collaboration feature in Orbit enables people to communicate on cloud or intranet using Instant Messaging, which in turn enhances the speed of project delivery. Collaboration is achieved by implementing two main sub-components of the feature: Threads and Snapshots.

 

Image annotations, which are used as comments on snapshots/images, enable quick action by the people involved in the project. Voting option in collaboration facilitates immediate feedback on project-related questions by casting votes. Collaboration also provides the ability to seamlessly upload and share documents/images using instant messaging.
