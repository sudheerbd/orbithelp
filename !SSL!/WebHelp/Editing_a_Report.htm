Editing a Report

To Edit a Report

 

1. Under the Reports area, select the required folder to view the reports under it.

2. Right-click the required report, and then click Edit.

      (or)                    

    For the required report, click icon, and then click Edit.

3. The required report opens in Edit Mode .

      

 

4. Edit/Update the report, and then click Save.

 

Browser Constraint: As opposed to other browser, in Internet Explorer, to edit the name of the report on the run result area, you must double-click the report name;

the name that appears unavailable initially, becomes available once it is double-clicked.
