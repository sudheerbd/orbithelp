Assigning Users to the Group

To assign a user to the group

 

  1. Under the Groups section, select the required Group, and then click  next to Assigned Users.

  2. A list of users appears (highlighted as "1").

                                                        

  1. Select the required user from the list.

  2. Under Assigned Users, the selected user appears.

          

                                        

  1. To save the changes, click Save.
