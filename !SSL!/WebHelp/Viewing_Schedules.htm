Viewing Schedules

To view the Schedules

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to   Monitoring, and then click Processes.

  2. Processes page appears with a list of processes.

  3. To view schedules page, click Schedules tab.

  4. Under Schedules tab a list of scheduled/upcoming schedules appears.

  1. To view the schedules under the currently logged in user, click My Schedules .

  2. To view all the schedules, click All Schedules .

  3. To refresh the submitted processes, click Refresh.
