Cancelling the Schedule

To cancel the schedule

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to   Monitoring, and then click Processes.

  2. Processes page appears with a list of processes.

  3. To view schedules page, click Schedules tab.

  4. To cancel a schedule, click  icon  (highlighted as “1”), and then click Cancel Schedule (highlighted as "2"). .

 

User can able to cancel a schedule, when the status is in Executing.

 

 

  1. A message “Are you sure you want to cancel the schedule" dialog appears.

  2. To cancel the schedule, click Yes.
