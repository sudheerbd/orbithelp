Scheduling a Job

To Schedule a Job

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to , and then click  under Actions.

  2. To schedule the required job, click Schedule.

  1. Schedule Job dialog appears as shown below:

 

  1. In the Schedule Job dialog, perform the following:

    1. To schedule the job immediately, select Run Immediately checkbox, and then click Submit.

    2. To schedule the job at a later time, remove the checkmark from Run Immediately checkbox, and then click Submit.

  1. The scheduled job appears under Monitoring -> Processes.
