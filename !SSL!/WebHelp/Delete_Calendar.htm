Delete Calendar

To delete a scheduler calendar

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to System, and then click Maintenance.

  2. Scheduler page appears with Operations tab.

  3. To view calendars list, click Calendars.

  4. To delete a calendar, click  icon (highlighted as “1”) , and then click Delete (highlighted as "2").

  5. Alert dialog appears as shown below:

  6. To delete the calendar, click Yes.
