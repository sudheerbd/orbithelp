Filtering a Domain

To filter a domain

 

  1. Under Domains & Data Models area, click .

  2. Textboxes to filter a domain appear under Name, Description, and Created By.

 

                                 

User can filter the domains using any one or combination of the following fields: Name, Description, and Created By.

  1. Under the required column, type the search term to filter.

  2. Based on the filter value, the search results appear as shown:

 

 

When the filter is active, filtered column name color changes to blue and font style changes to italics.    

  1. To clear the filter, clear search term in each field or click  on the filter toolbar.

  2. To hide the filter toolbar, click .

Clicking  only shows/hides the filter toolbar. Clicking does not clear the filter.

  1. To refresh the list of domain, click Refresh .
