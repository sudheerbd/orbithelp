Viewing Processes

To view the processes

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to   Monitoring, and then click Processes.

  2. Under Processes sections the following tab appears

    • Processes

    • Schedule

By default, Processes tab appears with a list of processes.

  1. Under Processes tab (highlighted as "1") a list of processes appears.

  2. To view the processes under the currently logged in user, click My processes.
  3. To view all the scheduled processes, click All processes.
  4. To refresh the submitted processes, click Refresh.
