Working with Single Sign-on

To work with Single Sign-on

 

  1. In the address bar, enter the https URL.

https://orbdev01.orbit8.com/reporting/

  1. Single Sign-on page appears.

Single sign-on page ends with sso/saml as shown below:

 

  1. Under the Sign In section, perform the following:

a.  In the Username box, enter the username.

b.   In the Password box, enter the password.

c.   To logon to the application, click Sign In.

 

Once you have logged in, the page navigates to the Orbit application as shown below:

 

 

If user need to login as an administrator, to a SAML configure instance. Add “bypass-saml-login-flow”.

https://orbdev01.orbit8.com/reporting/bypass-saml-login-flow
