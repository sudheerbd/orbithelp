Deleting Datasets

 

To delete a dataset

 

  1. In the Datasets area, a list of available datasets appears.                           

  2. For the required dataset, under Actions, click  icon, and then click Remove.  

 

 

  1. A confirmation message “Are you sure you want to delete selected dataset?” appears.

 

       

  1. Click Yes to delete the dataset.

  2. Click No to close the dialog box
