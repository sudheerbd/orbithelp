Removing a Job

To remove a job

 

1. On the left-hand side menu, point to , and then click Jobs.

2. Jobs section appears with a list of available jobs.

3. To delete the required job, select icon, under Actions and then click Remove.

 

Remove option is available for Custom Jobs.

4. A message " Are you sure you want to delete selected job" appears.

5. To delete the job, click Yes.
