Renaming a Domain

To Rename a Domain

  1. In the Domain & Data Models section, under Actions, click .

  2. To rename a domain, click Rename.

  3. Rename- <Domain Name> dialog box appears.

  4. In the Rename- <Domain Name> dialog box, perform the following:

    • In the Name box, rename the domain with a new one.

    • In the Description box, edit the description.

  5. To save the changes, click Save.

  6. To close the dialog, click Cancel or .
