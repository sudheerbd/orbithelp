Scheduling a Report

To schedule a Report

 

  1. Under the Reports area, the required folder appears with its associated reports.

  2. Right-click the required report, and then click Schedule.

(OR)

            For the selected report, click icon , and then click Schedule.

 

   

 

  1. Schedule - Report dialog box appears.

 

 

  1. Schedule dialog box has the following options:

a.      Parameters. See Parameters  (highlighted as “1”)

b.      Schedule  See Schedule (highlighted as “2”)

c.       Output & Delivery See Output & Delivery (highlighted as “3”)

d.      Notifications See Notifications (highlighted as “4”)

 

  

  1. To schedule the report, fill the required fields in the above mentioned tabs, and then click Submit. See Monitoring.
