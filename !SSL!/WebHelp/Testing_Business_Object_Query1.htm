Testing Business Object Query

To test Business Object query

 

  1. Beside the Domain Hierarchy area, Semantic Layers area appears.

  2. In the Semantic Layers area, the domain menu appears.

  3. In the domain menu, click Test Query (highlighted as “1”).  

  4. Test Query area (highlighted as “2”) appears in the Semantic Layers area.

 

 

 

  1. Test Query is divided into the following three sections:

    1. Business Objects area (highlighted as “3”)

    2. Selected Columns area (highlighted as “4”)

    3. Query area (highlighted as “5”)  

 

 

  1. Under the Business Objects area, in the Select Business Object box, type the required business object or click downward arrow  to search the required BO (highlighted as “6”) from the available list.

 

       

                                                          

  1. The required business object appears.

  2. Under the business object, table/view/synonym appears with columns under it.

  3. Drag and drop the required column on to the Selected Column area. 

 

 

  1. Under Selected Columns area, Aggregation and Column Name of the selected column appear.

  2. When the columns are dragged onto to the Selected Columns area, a query is automatically generated under Query section as in the above figure.

  3. To remove the columns from the Selected Column area, click Remove selected rows.

  4. Under the Selected Columns area, Conditions and Order By areas appear.

  5. To show the results of the SQL query, click Show results.

  6. To refresh the query, click  Refresh Query.

  7. To copy the sql query, click Copy Sql Query
