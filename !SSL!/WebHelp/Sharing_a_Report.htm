Sharing a Report

To Share a Report

 

1. Under the Reports area, the required folder appears with its associated reports.

2. Right-click the required report, and then click Share.

(OR)

   For the selected report, click icon , and then click Share.

         

3. Share Report - <report name> dialog box appears.

 

     

4. Sharing Reports is done in the following two ways:

a.  On Assigned Roles, click  to add a role.

b . A list of available roles appears.

c. Select the required role.

d. The required role appears under Assigned Roles.

e. This shares the report to the required assigned roles.

Required Role: ORBIT USER ROLE

           

(OR)

a.  On Assigned Users, click  to add a user.

b . A list of available users appears.

c. Select the required user.

d. The required user appears under Assigned Users.      

e. This shares the report to the required users (highlighted as “2”). 

Required User: XXFORX

            

             

5. To delete the assigned user/role, select the required user/role and then click  Delete User/Delete Role respectively.

6. To save the changes, click Ok.
