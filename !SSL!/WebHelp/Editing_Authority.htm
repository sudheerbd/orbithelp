Editing Authority

To edit authority

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to  User Management, and then click  Authorities.

  2. Authorities list appears as shown below:

 

  1. To edit the required authorities, click icon (highlighted as “1”) under Actions, and then click Edit (highlighted as “2”).

 

  1. Edit Authority dialog box appears.

 

  1. Edit the required fields, and then click Save to make the changes.

  1. To close the dialog box, click Cancel.
