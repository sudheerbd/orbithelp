Process Finance Cube Batch

This primarily imports the data into the finance cubes

 

Prerequisites: You must have set the Finance Cubes and Data Loads.

    1. On the left-hand side menu, point to , and then click Jobs.

    2. Jobs page appears with a list of Seeded and Custom jobs.

    3. Under Jobs sections, on Process Finance Cube Batch, click , and then click Schedule.

    4. Schedule Job dialog appears.

 

    1. In the Schedule Job dialog, on the Parameters tab, perform the following:

  • In the Finance Cubes box, type/select the required finance cubes

  • In the Data Loads box, type/select the required data loads.

Data Loads depend on the selected Finance Cube.

 

 

  • To schedule the job, click Submit.
