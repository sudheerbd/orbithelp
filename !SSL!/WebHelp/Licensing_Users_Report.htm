Licensing Users Report

This feature generates a report stating the number of licenses for a user account.  

To License Users Report

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, click Business Units.

  2. Business Units  section appears with a list of available business units.

  3. For a license users report, click icon (highlighted as “1”) under Actions, and then click  License Users Report.

 

 

  1. License Users Report dialog box appears.

  2. To get a license users report, click Yes.

 

  1. To cancel the dialog, click  or No.  

When "Yes" is clicked, a success message  "Mail Sent Successfully!" appears.

  1. The license users report is sent to Email configured in the respective business unit.
