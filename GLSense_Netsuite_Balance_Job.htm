GLSense NetSuite Balance Job

This job primarily extracts all the GL Balance data from NetSuite instance.

This job is similar to the job GLSense Fusion Balance Job. For detailed information see GLSense Fusion Balance Job.

Netsuite Balance Job has an additional option "Custom Segents". But it is not required to add custom segments in the balance job.
If user wants to add custom segments, add them in the GLSense Netsuite Setup Job. Refer to GLSense Netsuite Setp Job for more details about custom segments.
