Sharing a Domain

To share a Domain

 

  1. In Domain & Data Models section, under Actions, click .

  2. To share a domain, click Share.

 

 

  1. Share- <Domain Name> dialog appears.

  1. In the Share- <Domain Name>, perform the following:

  1. On Assigned Roles, click  to add a role.

  2. A list of available roles appears.

  3. Select the required role.

  4. The required role appears under Assigned Roles.

  5. This shares the report to the required assigned roles.

Required Role: ORBIT Super USER ROLE

     (OR)

    1. On Assigned Users, click  to add a user.

    2. A list of available users appears.

    3. Select the required user.

    4. The required user appears under Assigned Users.      

    5. This shares the report to the required users.  

   Required User: test

             

5. To delete the assigned user/role, select the required user/role and then click  Delete User/Delete Role respectively.

6. To save the changes, click Ok.

7. To close the dialog, click Close or .
