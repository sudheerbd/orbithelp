Domain Actions

To view the actions for domain, perform the following:

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to Business Models, and then click Domains.

  2. Domains & Data Models window appears.

  3. Under the Domain & Data Models, the available domains appear as shown below:

 

 

  1. In the Domains & Data Models section, under Actions, click  (highlighted as "1").

  2. The following options appear:

    • Edit

    • Remove

    • Rename

    • Export

    • Share

    • Copy

Actions options will be available only to the owner (Created By) of the Domains & Data Models.
