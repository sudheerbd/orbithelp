Opening the Closed Thread

To Opena thread

 

1. In the Threads section, under Actions, click  against the thread you want to open, and then click Open.

 

2.Status Change dialog box appears.

3. To open the thread, click Yes in Status Change dialog box.

 

Note: A Collaboration thread status change request processed notification appears briefly at the top of the ORBIT application window.

4. To cancel open thread request, click No.
