Importing Domain/Report

 

To Import a Domain

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to , and then click Jobs.

  2. Jobs page appears with a list of Seeded and Custom jobs.

  3. Under Jobs sections, on Import Domain/Report, click , and then click Schedule.

  4. Schedule Job dialog appears.

  5. In the Schedule Job dialog, on the Parameters tab, click Upload to upload the required xml file of domain that must be imported, and then click Submit.

 

  

 

  1. A success message appears once the domain is imported, and a new process is created under Processes under Monitoring.
