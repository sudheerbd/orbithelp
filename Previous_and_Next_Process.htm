Previous Process

To view previous processes of an scheduled process

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to Monitoring, and then click Processes.

  2. Processes page appears with a list of processes.

  3. To view previous process, click icon (highlighted as “1”), and then click Previous Process (highlighted as "2").

    Previous Process option is available, when the processes is in a repeat state.
