Configuring Email Settings

To Configure Email Settings

1.      On the Security menu, click Settings.

 

        

                       Figure: Displaying Settings

 

 

2.      Email tab appears by default.

3.      To create a new email setting, click New .

4.      Textboxes (highlighted as “1”) appear under Name, UserName, Password, ServerName, Port, and Security.

 

        

                                           Figure : Creating New Email Setting

 

5.      Under the Email tab, perform the following:

·         In the Name box, type the name for the email setting.

·         In the UserName box, type the user name for the email setting.

·         In the Password box, type the password.

·         In the ServerName box, type the server name.

·         In the Port box, type the port number.

·         In the Security list, click the required option.

·         To save the fields, click Save.

6.      To delete the required email setting, click Delete (highlighted as “2”).

 

       

                                                         Figure: Deleting the Email Setting

 

 

7.      To edit/update the required email settings, double-click on the required row. 

8.      Edit/make the changes, and then click Update.
