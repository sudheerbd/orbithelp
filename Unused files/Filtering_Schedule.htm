Filtering Schedule

To filter schedules

 

  1. Under Schedules tab area, click filter.

  2. Textboxes to filter the processes appear under Schedule Id, Name, Status, Start Time, End Time, Next Run At  and Submitted By.

 

 

  1. Under the required field, type the required search term (highlighted as “1”).
