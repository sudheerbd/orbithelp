FTL Template for TXT Output

The following is the FTL Template for TXT output.

 

<#assign tabledata = Root.bands.ReportData>

<#list tabledata as Cname>

${Cname.fields.COL0};${Cname.fields.COL1};${Cname.fields.COL2}

</#list>
