Enable/Disable Auto Interaction

To enable/disable auto interaction

 

  1. Create a new dashboard, and drag and drop the relevant reports from the Widgets sections onto the dashboard work area (highlighted as “1”).

 

Note: All the reports dropped onto the workspace must have been built on the same Business Object.

 

 

  1. When auto interaction is enabled among the reports, all the reports displayed above interact with each other.

 

 

To Enable Auto interaction

  1. On the top menu, click Options, and then click Enable/Disable auto interaction.

To disable the auto interaction

 

  1. On the top menu, click Options, and then click Enable/Disable auto interaction (which is already enabled). 

 

Note: Since the Enable/Disable auto interaction has been enabled earlier, it can be disabled by selecting it again.

This removes the checkmark before Enable/Disable auto interaction.
