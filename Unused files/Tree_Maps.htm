Widget Settings of Tree Maps, Sun Burst and Heat Maps

The widgets settings of tree maps, sun burst, and heat map are same. The following tabs appears under the widget settings:

 

To set the Visual settings

1.      On the report edit page, click Visual Settings.

2.      Under the Visual Settings the following tabs appear:

a.       General

b.      Value Axis

 

General

1.      Under the General tab, perform the following:

a.       In the Title box, type the required title.

Example: Title: Tee Map

 

Value Axis

1.      Under the Value Axis, perform the following:

a.       In the Scale box, type/select the required option from the following

                                i.            None

                              ii.            Thousands

                            iii.            Millions

                            iv.            Billions

                              v.            Trillions
