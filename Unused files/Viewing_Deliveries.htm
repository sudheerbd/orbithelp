Viewing Deliveries

To view the delivery for a report

1.      Under Scheduled Processes, select the required report.

2.      For the required report, delivery is displayed under Deliveries area. 

3.      Under Deliveries area, the following delivery options:

·         Email

·         FTP

·         Fax

¥    The delivery for a specific report appears in this section, only when the delivery option is set in Output & Delivery tab in Schedule dialog box.

¥    To view the schedule option, right-click the required report, and then click Schedule.

¥    To set the delivery option, see Setting Delivery

 

4.      To refresh the delivery, click Refresh.
