Editing Custom Widgets

To edit the custom widget

 

1. Open the required analysis report.

2. In the report configurator area, click Change Widget.

3. The newly created custom widget appears.

      Example: Newly created custom:

4. To edit the custom widget, right-click the required custom widget, and then click Edit.

5. The selected custom widget dialog appears.

6. Make the required changes and then click Save.

7. To discard the changes, click Cancel.
