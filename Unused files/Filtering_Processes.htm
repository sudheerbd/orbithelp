Filtering Processes

To filter processes

 

  1. Under Processes area, click filter.

  2. Textboxes to filter the processes appear under Process Id, Name, Phase,  Status, Start Time and End Time.

 

 

  1. Under the required field, type the required search term (highlighted as “1”). 

  2. Based on the filter, the search results (highlighted as “2”) appear as shown:

 

 

  1. To refresh the process tab, click Refresh.
