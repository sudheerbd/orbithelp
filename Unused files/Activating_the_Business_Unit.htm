Activating the Business Unit

To activate the business unit

 

  1. Under the Business Units area, in the STATUS column, the current status of the required business unit appears as Disabled.

  2. To enable the business unit, click Disabled icon for the required customer.

  3. Status Change dialog box appears with a message “Are you sure you want to enable selected Business Unit?

       

          

Figure: Displaying Status Change Message

 

  1. To continue, click Yes.

  2. To discard the message, click No.

  3. A success message “Business Unit enabled successfully” appears.
