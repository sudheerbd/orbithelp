Deleting Custom Widgets

To delete the custom widget

 1. Open the required analysis report.

2. In the report configurator area, click Change Widget.

3. The newly created custom widget appears.

      Example: Newly created custom:

4. To delete the custom widget, right-click the required custom widget, and then click Delete.

5. Delete Custom Widget dialog box appears with a message  “Are you sure you want to delete this widget? ”.

6. Click Yes, to delete the widget.

7. To discard the changes, click No.
