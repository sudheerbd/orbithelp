Creating a Business Object

 

Once the Logical model is created, the user needs to create a Business Object. A Business Object is an intermediary between the domain and the reports.

 

To create a New Business Object

 

1.      Under Logical Models, right-click the Business Objects, and click New Business Object.

 

2.      New Business Object dialog box appears.

 

 

 

3.   To create a New Business Object, perform the following:

·   In the Name box, type the name of the business object.

·   In the Description box, type the description for the business object.

·   To save the business object, click Save.

·   To close the dialog box, click Cancel.  

 

 

4.      Newly created business object appears as shown below:

 

 

5.      Right-click the newly created business object, and then click Open.

6.      Two Sections – Logical Tables <Business Object Name> (highlighted as “1”) and Business Objects (highlighted as “1”) – appear in the work area. 

 

7.      Under Business Objects, right-click the newly created business objects, and then click New Category.

8.      New Category option appears underneath the Business Object.

 

 

 

¥   Categories are created to organize the reports/objects.

 

9.      To group the related columns under one category, drag the required column from the Logical tables on the left hand-side, and drop them in the related categories.
