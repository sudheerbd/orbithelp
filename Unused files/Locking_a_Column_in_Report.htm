Locking a Column in Report

Locking option freezes the column that enables to compare the specific column with remaining columns in the report. Horizontal scrollbar for the locked column appears grayed and cannot be moved.

To lock the column

1.      In the Run Results area, move the cursor over the required column.

2.      Downward arrow  appears beside the column name.

3.      To lock the column in a report, click  beside the required name, and then click Lock (highlighted as “1”).

 

                                                     

4.      The locked column (highlighted as “2”) appears on the left-hand side of the report.

       

       

                                  

 

       5.    The horizontal scrollbar option appears grayed and is unavailable to move.
