Inserting Excel Edge in Excel

To insert Excel Edge app in Excel

 

1.      Launch the Excel Work book.

2.      On the Insert tab, in the Add-ins group, click Store (highlighted as “1”).

 

 

3.      Office Add-ins window appears.

4.      In the Office Add-ins window, on the SHARED FOLDER tab, select the ORBIT Excel Edge application, and then click Add.

    

        

 

5.      ORBIT Excel Edge application appears in the Excel workbook on the right-hand side.
