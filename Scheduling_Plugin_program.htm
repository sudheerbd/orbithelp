Scheduling Plugin Program

To schedule a plugin program

Register plugin program will appears under Jobs section, as job type Plugin.

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to , and then click Jobs.

  2. A list of register program appears as shown below:

  1. To schedule a plugin job, click  under Actions and then select Schedule.

  2. The scheduled job appears under Monitor section.
