Getting Started with XLEdge

To get started with XLEdge

ORBIT XLEdge supports Microsoft Excel 2013 or higher.

  1. Navigate to the ORBIT Analytics URL.

  2. In the ORBIT Analytics Login Page, perform the following:

    1. In the Username box, type the required username.

    2. In the Password box, type the required password.

    3. To login to the ORBIT Analytics application, click Login.

    4. ORBIT Analytics Home page appears.

    5. On the top menu, click the icon or icon, on the top right-hand side of the window.

    6. To download XEedge MSI file, click Download Addons (highlighted as “1”).
