Running a Report

 

To run a Report

 

  1. Under the Reports area, the following options appear on the left-hand side:

  • Quick Access

  • My Folders

  • Shared Folders

 

  1. From the available folders, select the required folder.

  2. The reports under the selected folder appear on the right-hand side.

  1. On the right-hand side, right-click the required report, and then click Open (highlighted as “1”).

(OR)

  1. For the selected report, click  , and then click Open.
