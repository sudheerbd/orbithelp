Sharing a report at Folder Level

 

   1. In the Reports section, click Options, and then click Share.

         2. Share Reports dialog appears with the following three tabs:

a.   Assigned Roles & Users

b.  Assign to Role

c.  Assign to User

 

 

Assigned Roles & Users

 

1.  Under the Assigned Roles & Users tab, the following three sections appear:

a.       Reports (highlighted as “1”)

b.      Assigned Roles (highlighted as “2”)

c.       Assigned Users (highlighted as “3”)

 

        

 

2. From the Reports section, select the required folder, sub folder or the individual report.

You can either assign a role or a user or both for the selected folder/report.

3. To assign the role, click Search for items to add.

4. From the available roles, select the required role.

ORBIT SUPER USER ROLE

5. The selected role appears under the Assigned Roles section.

6. To assign the user, click Search for items to add.

7. From the available users, select the required users.

TestuserD

8. The selected user appears under the Assigned Users section.

  • For example, considering the above scenario, if the a report is shared to ORBIT SUPER USER ROLE role, and "TestuserD" user, then the report will be available to all the users having the ORBIT SUPER USER ROLE role irrespective of the user and the report will also be available to "TestuserD" user.

9. The Assigned Roles/Assigned Users can Run, Edit, Delete, Copy, Schedule, Export, Generate Link and Allow Insights.

  • Run and Schedule options are available to the Assigned Roles/Assigned Users, by default.

10. To select the other options (Edit Delete, Copy, Export , Generate Link and Allow Insights), select the checkbox below the required options below.      

11. To share the folders to the subfolders of a main folder, select the Apply to subfolder checkbox.

12. To save the changes, click Save.

 

 

Assign to Role

 

1. Under the Assign to Role tab, the following two sections appear:

a.       Roles (highlighted as “1”)

b.      Assign Reports (highlighted as “2”)

 

  

 

2. Under Roles, the available roles appear.

3. Under Assign Reports, the available reports appear.

4. Under Roles, select the required role.

5. The reports under the specific role appears under the Assign Reports section.

6. Select the required report, then perform the required actions(Run, Edit, Delete, Copy, Schedule, Export , Generate Link and Allow Insights).

7. To save the changes, click Save.

 

 

 

8. To show only the shared reports, click the Show Shared Reports Only checkbox (highlighted as “3”) on Assign Reports section.

9. To show all the reports, deselect the Show All check box (highlighted as “3”) on the Assign Reports section.

10. To search the reports, click Search (highlighted as “4”).  

 

Assign to User

 

1. Under the Assign to User tab, the following two sections appear:

a.       Users (highlighted as “1”)

b.      Assign Reports (highlighted as “2”)

   

 

2. Under Users, the available users appear.

3. Under Assign Reports, the available reports appear.

4. Under Users, select the required user.

5. The reports under the specific user appears under the Assign Reports section.

6. Select the required report, then perform the required actions (Run, Edit, Delete, Copy, Schedule, Export, Generate Link and Allow Angles).

7. To save the changes, click Save.

 

 

8. To show only the shared reports, click the Show Shared Reports Only checkbox (highlighted as “3”) on Assign Reports section.

9. To show all the reports, deselect the Show All check box (highlighted as “3”) on the Assign Reports section.

10. To search the reports, click Search (highlighted as “4”).
