Deleting a Report

To Delete a Report

 

1. Under the Reports area, select the required folder to view the reports under it.

2. Right-click the required report, and then click Delete.

      (or)

3. For the required report, clickicon, and then click Delete.

 

        

4. A confirmation message “Are you sure you want to delete this report?” appears.

 

5. To delete the report, click Yes. 

6. The deleted report does not appear under selected folder in Reports section.
