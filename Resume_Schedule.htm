Resuming the Schedule

To resume the schedule

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to   Monitoring, and then click Processes.

  2. Processes page appears with a list of processes.

  3. To view schedules page, click Schedules tab.

  4. To resume a paused schedules, click  icon (highlighted as “1”) , and then click Resume (highlighted as "2").

 

Resume option available for paused schedules
