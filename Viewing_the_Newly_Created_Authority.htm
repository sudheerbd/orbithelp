Viewing the Newly Created Authority

To view the newly created authority

 

  1. In the ORBIT Login page, perform the following:

a.       In the Username box, type the username of the user (highlighted as “1”) under the newly created authority.

b.      In the Password box, type the password of the user (highlighted as “2”), and then click Sign In.  

 

        

  1. The Reports page appears for the user.

  2. All the reports (highlighted as “3”) from other datasources would be imported to My Folders under the reports area.

 

                    

 

The user under the newly created authority can now access all the features of ORBIT application.
