Visualization

To add a visualization widget in the dashboard, perform the following:

 

  1. On the top-menu, from the Add Widget (highlighted as "1") list, select Visualization (highlighted as "2").

 

 

  1. A list of reports appears, under Add Visualization dialog.

  2. To add the visualization to the report, click (highlighted as 3) as show below:

 

Note:

    • All the available analysis reports appears under Add Visualization dialog.

    • A "Visualization added to dashboard" notification appears briefly at the top of the ORBIT application window.

  1. To close the dialog, click Done or   Close dialog.
