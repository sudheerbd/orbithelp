Archiving a Thread

To archive a thread

 

Note: To archive a thread, the thread must be in “Locked/Closed” state.

1.    In the Threads section, under Actions, click  against the thread you want to archive, and then click Archive. 

    

 

2.    Archive Thread dialog appears.        

3.    To archive the thread, click Yes.

4.   To cancel /close the dialog, click No/   Close dialog.

 

Alternate Procedure    

1.    In the ORBIT application, at the top of the page, click  Collaboration Threads.

2.   A window appears with a list of collaboration threads.

3.    From the list of threads, select the thread you want to archive.

Note: In the collaboration threads list, thread status indicator appears grey for locked threads.

4.    In the chat window of the selected thread, click  Thread Chat Settings Menu, and then click Archive Thread.

5. Archive Thread dialog box appears.

6. To archive the thread, click Yes.

Note: A Collaboration thread status change request processed notification appears briefly at the top of the ORBIT application window.

7.   To cancel archive thread request, click No.
