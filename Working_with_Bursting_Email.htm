Working with Bursting Email

To work with Bursting for Email

 

1. Open the required data report.

2. On the right-hand side top, click   Options (highlighted as “1”), and then click Delivery Plans ( highlighted as “2”).

 

   

3. Delivery Plans dialog appears.

 

     

4. To create a new delivery plan, click New Delivery Plan.

5. The following figure appears with available fields.

    

   

6. In the Plan Name box, type the required delivery plan name.

Plan Name: Email Bursting

7. In the Description box, type the required description.

Description: This is test delivery plan.

Description is an optional field.

8. In the Delivery Type box, select the required delivery type from the following:

  • EMAIL

  • FTP

  • FILE

Required Delivery Type: EMAIL

9. To maintain the history of the delivery plan, select the Maintain History checkbox.


By default, Maintain History checkbox will be selected. If you don’t want to maintain history, uncheck Maintain History checkbox.
When Maintain History checkbox is checked, the history appears in Processes.

10. In the Split/Delivery By box, drag the required field from the business object section.

Since, bursting is done region-wise, drag Region from the business object section.

Split/Delivery By : Region

11.  In the Report Mapping Field list, select the required field that needs to be mapped to the field with which the bursting is done.

Since, bursting is done region-wise, and “Sample-Superstore”. “Region” appears in the Split/Delivery By box, the Report Mapping Fieid must be mapped appropriately.

For a Publisher Report, the Report Mapping Fields are considered from the first band, first query columns.

Report Mapping Field: Region

 

   

 

12. Under Email tab, perform the following:

  •  In the From box, drag and drop the required field from the business object or type the required email by clicking .

  • To use Calculation Formula, refer to Using Calculation Formula.

  • In the To box, drag and drop the required field from the business object or type the required email by clicking .

  • In the CC box, drag and drop the required field from the business object or type the required email by clicking .

  • In the BCC box, drag and drop the required field from the business object or type the required email by clicking .

  • In the Subject box, drag and drop the required field from the business object or type the required subject by clicking .

Subject: Test Email

  • In the Body box, drag and drop the required field from the business object or type the required body by clicking .

Body: This is a test email.

  • To send the report as an attachment, select Attachment radio button.

  • To send the report as a Link, select Link radio button.

  • In the File Name box, drag and drop the required field from the business object or type the required body by clicking .

  • In the Zip list, click the required option from the following:

  • False

  • True

  • In the Password box, drag and drop the required field from the business object or type the required password by clicking .

  • Based on selected fields, a query is formed which appears in the Query box as shown below:

  

     

13. To show the query result, click Show results.

14. To refresh the query, click Refresh Query.

15. To save the changes, click Save.

16. To cancel any updates, click Cancel.
