Exporting a Custom Report

To Export a custom report

 

  1. Under the Reports area, select the required folder to view the reports under it.

  2. Right-click the required report, and then click Export.

(OR)

          For the selected report, click icon , and then click Export.

  1. Expoprt- Report dialog box appears.

 

 

  1. To export custom report, click Submit .
