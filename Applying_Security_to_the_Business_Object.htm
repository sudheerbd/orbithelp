Applying Security to the Business Object

 You can apply security to the business object by assigning the roles/users to the selected business object.  Here, in this scenario we are applying security by assigning roles to the selected business object.

To apply security to the Business Object

 

   1.           On the left-hand side menu, point to Business Models and then click Security.

   2.           Security page appears with two tabs:

a.     Business Objects

b.     Jobs

 

3.      On the Business Objects tab, select the Assign Roles & Users sub-tab.

4.      A list of available business objects appears.

5.      Select the required business object , and then click Search for items to add beside Assigned Roles on the right-hand side.

6.      A list of available roles appears.

7.      From the available roles, select the required role .

8.      The selected role appears under Assigned Roles . 

9.      To save the changes, click Save.
