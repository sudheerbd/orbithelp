Next Process

To view next process of an scheduled process

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to Monitoring, and then click Processes.

  2. Processes page appears with a list of processes.

  3. To view next process, click icon (highlighted as “1”), and then click Next Process (highlighted as "2").

    Next Process option is available, when the processes is in a repeat state.

    To navigate back to processes list, select My Processes/ All Processes.

To view next Process from Previous Process

  1. To view previous process, click  icon  (highlighted as “1”), and then click Previous Process (highlighted as "2").

    In the above process, Next Process option is not available.

  2. Page navigates to Previous Process window.

  3. To view next process, click  icon  (highlighted as “3”), and then click Next Process (highlighted as "4").

    Next Process option becomes unavailable, when repeat schedule processes ends.
