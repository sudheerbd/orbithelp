Exporting Reports

To export reports

 

  1. On the top right-hand corner of the run results area, click Options, point to Export, and then click the required format for exporting.

  2. In ORBIT Analytics application, reports can be exported in the following format:

    • Excel xlsx

    • With Template

(OR)

  1. On the top menu, click Export to Excel xlsx,on the top right-hand side of the window

                                     

 
i. A grouping report can export with Excel xlsx only; grouping does not work With Template.
    (OR)
   A grouping report can export from report schedule using output type as Excel xlsx.
ii. A grouping report can not export to other output formats
