Searching Schedules

To search schedules with Schedule Id

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to   Monitoring, and then click Processes.

  2. Processes page appears with a list of processes.

  3. To view schedules page, click Schedules tab.

  4. To search a schedule with schedule id, enter the required schedule id in Schedule Id Search.

  5. Schedule Id appears as shown below:

 

 

  1. To search the schedule with advance option, click Advanced Monitoring Search next to Schedule Id Search.

  2. To reset the entered search options, click Reset.
