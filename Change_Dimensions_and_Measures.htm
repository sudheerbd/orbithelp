Change Dimensions and Measures

To change the dimensions and measures

  1. To view visualization option, click Options (highlighted as "1") on visualization tab.

(OR)

Right-click on the visualization widget.

  1. A list of options appears, on visualization widget as shown below (highlighted as "2").

 

 

  1. To change the dimensions and measures on the existing visualization, right-click on the  visualization and then click Change Dimensions & Measures.

 

To work with dimensions and measures perform the following

 

  1. To work with Change Dimensions & Measures, we should have alternative dimensions and measures.

  2. To add alternative dimensions and measure perform the following

    • Navigate to report Data section and select the required Axis field to add fields

    • To add the required fields in measures field, click next to Values field and then select Alternative Measures (highlighted as "3").

 

 

    • To add the required fields in dimensions field, click next to Category or Color field and then select Alternative Dimensions.

    • The added Alternative Measures and Dimensions (highlighted as "4" and "5") appears as shown below:

 

 

To view the dimensions and measures on visualization perform the following

 

  1. Right-click on the required visualization widget and then select Change Dimensions & Measures.
