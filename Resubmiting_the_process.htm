Resubmitting the process

To resubmit the process

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to Monitoring, and then click Processes.

  2. Processes page appears with a list of processes.

  3. To resubmit the process, click icon (highlighted as “1”), and then click Run Again (highlighted as "2").

  4. Schedule window appears as shown below:

  5. To submit the processes, apply the required changes and then click Submit.

  6. A new processes appears under the Processes tab.
