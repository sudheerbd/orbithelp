Creating New Category

To create a new category

 

  Here, the New Category is created in Datasets. Similarly, New Category can  be created in domain as well.

 

1.      Under the Dataset Hierarchy , Business Objects folder appears. 

2.      In the Business Objects folder, right-click the required business object that needs to be opened, and then click Open.

 

          

                       

 

3.      Two sections— Logical Tables and Business Objects appear.

4.      The required business object (highlighted as “1”) appears on the extreme right-hand side of the workspace area.

 

                                           

5. To create a new category, right-click the required business object and then click New Category.

 

     

6. New Category folder appears as shown below:

 

7. Provide a suitable name to the New category folder.

 

8. Move the columns from the Logical Tables section to the newly created category folder under Business Objects section.

   Example: Newly Created Category Folder : Superstore category

 

   

9. To save the category folder, click Save.

10. The category folder of a business object appears in the Business Objects area in the Reports (highlighted as "3"),when that specific business object is selected.
