Scheduling a Custom Report

To Schedule a Custom Report

 

  1. Under the Reports area, select the required folder to view the reports under it.

  2. Right-click the required report, and then click Schedule.

(OR)

            For the selected report, click icon , and then click Schedule.

  1. Schedule - Report dialog box appears.

  2. In the Schedule -Report dialog, schedule the report, by assigning the parameters and configuring notifications, and then click Submit.
