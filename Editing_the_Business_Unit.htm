Editing the Business Unit

To edit the business unit

 

  1. On the menu bar, click Business Units.

  2. Business Units page appears, as shown below:

 

  1. In the Business Units page, the existing business units appear.

  2. To edit a business unit, click icon (highlighted as “1”) under Actions, and then click Edit (highlighted as “2”).

 

  1. Edit Business Unit - <Name > dialog box appears.

 

                                             

  1. Edit the required fields, and then click Save.

 



i. You cannot edit the fields: Admin Username.
ii. Name key which is used to generate the links for activation emails is not allowing (-) Hyphen whereas (-) Hyphen valid is a symbol for a host name as long as it is not in the first or last position.
iii. Description, Banner Text, and Copyright Text fields are optional.
iv. You can add a logo by clicking Browse.
