Show Processes

To show the processes of a schedule

 

  1. On the left-hand side menu, point to   Monitoring, and then click Processes.

  2. Processes page appears with a list of processes.

  3. To view schedules page, click Schedules tab.

  4. To view the related processes of the schedule, click  icon (highlighted as “1”) , and then click Show Processes (highlighted as "2").
