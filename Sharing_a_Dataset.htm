Sharing a Dataset

 

To share a  Dataset

  1.  In the Datasets section, a list of available datasets appears.

  2. For the required dataset, under Actions, click  icon, and then click Share.

  3. Share <dataset> dialog appears.

 

    

 

  1. To assign a required role to the dataset, perform the following:

  1. On the Assigned Roles section, click  Search for items to add.

  2. A list of available roles appears as shown below:    

 

 

  1. From the available roles, select the checkbox before the required role.

  2. Under Assigned Roles, the selected role appears.


Based on the assigned role, the following actions are available:
a. Create Report
b. Edit
c. Delete
d. Refresh
e. Upload File

     

By default, some of the actions are available based on the role; you can assign the required actions based on the requirements.

  1. To assign a required user to the dataset, perform the following:

  1. On the Assigned Users section, click  Search for items to add.

  2. A list of available users appears as shown below:    

 

  1. From the available users, select the checkbox before the required user.

  2. Under Assigned Users, the selected user appears.


Based on the assigned user, the following actions are available:
a. Create Report
b. Edit
c. Delete
d. Refresh
e. Upload File

By default, some of the actions are available based on the user assigned; you can assign the required actions based on the requirements.

  1. To save the changes, click Ok.

  1. To cancel the changes, click Cancel.
