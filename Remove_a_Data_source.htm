Removing a Data source

To Remove a Data Source

 

  1. In the Data Sources page, a list of available data sources appears.

  2. In the Data Sources page, under Actions, click  against the thread you want to remove, and then click Remove.

  3. Delete dialog box appears.

 

  1. To remove the data source, click Yes.

A Datasource deleted successfully notification appears briefly at the top of the ORBIT application window.

  1. To cancel the process, click No.
