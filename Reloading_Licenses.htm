Reloading Licenses

To Reload Licenses

 

  1. On the Business Units section, on the right-hand side, click  Reload Licenses (highlighted as "1").

 

 

  1. Reload Licenses dialog box appears as shown below:

  1. To reload the licenses, click Yes.

  2. A message "Licenses reloaded successfully" appears.

This reloads licenses of all the existing business units.

  1. To cancel the reload option, click No.

  1. To refresh the licenses, click Refresh.
