Applying Multiple Aggregations

To apply multiple aggregations

 

  1. Open the required report and then run it.

  2. Apply grouping on the required column.

    • Example:  Required column: State

  3. To apply multiple aggregations on a required report, perform the following:

    •  In the Configurator section, under Columns, click  for the required column, and then click Properties.

    •  Properties dialog box appears.

    • In the Properties dialog box, on the right-hand side, on the Grouping & Summary tab, select the required aggregations from the Summary box.

 

 

    • Example: required aggregations: SUM, AVG, MIN, MAX

    • To save the changes, click Ok.

  1. To view the changes, click Run.

  2. The report with applied aggregations appears as followed:

  1. To save the changes, click Save.
